## Test Structure

The CELPIP-General test has 4 sections completed in one sitting (~3 hours).

## 1. Listening (47-55 minutes)

| Part | Task | Questions |
|------|------|-----------|
| 1 | Listening to Problem Solving | 8 |
| 2 | Listening to a Daily Life Conversation | 5 |
| 3 | Listening for Information | 6 |
| 4 | Listening to a News Item | 5 |
| 5 | Listening to a Discussion | 8 |
| 6 | Listening to Viewpoints | 6 |

**Key Points:**
- Audio plays ONCE only
- Canadian/North American accents
- Multiple choice questions

## 2. Reading (55-60 minutes)

| Part | Task | Questions |
|------|------|-----------|
| 1 | Reading Correspondence | 11 |
| 2 | Reading to Apply a Diagram | 8 |
| 3 | Reading for Information | 9 |
| 4 | Reading for Viewpoints | 10 |

**Key Points:**
- Mix of practical and academic texts
- Time management is critical
- Some questions have drop-down menus

## 3. Writing (53-60 minutes)

| Task | Description | Time |
|------|-------------|------|
| Task 1 | Writing an Email | 27 minutes |
| Task 2 | Responding to Survey Questions | 26 minutes |

**Key Points:**
- On-screen typing
- Word count suggestions (not strict limits)
- Spell check available

## 4. Speaking (15-20 minutes)

| Task | Description | Prep | Speaking |
|------|-------------|------|----------|
| 1 | Giving Advice | 30s | 90s |
| 2 | Talking About Personal Experience | 30s | 60s |
| 3 | Describing a Scene | 30s | 60s |
| 4 | Making Predictions | 30s | 60s |
| 5 | Comparing and Persuading | 60s | 60s |
| 6 | Dealing with a Difficult Situation | 60s | 60s |
| 7 | Expressing Opinions | 30s | 90s |
| 8 | Describing an Unusual Situation | 30s | 60s |

**Key Points:**
- Speak into headset microphone
- Recorded responses
- No interaction with examiner

## Test Day Tips

1. **Arrive early** - 30 minutes before
2. **Bring valid ID** - Government-issued photo ID
3. **Get comfortable** - Adjust chair, headset, screen
4. **Use all your time** - Don't rush, don't leave early
