## What is CELPIP?

**CELPIP** (Canadian English Language Proficiency Index Program) is a computer-based English language test that measures your ability to communicate in English in everyday situations.

## Why CELPIP?

CELPIP is accepted for:

- **Permanent Residency** - Express Entry, Provincial Nominee Programs
- **Canadian Citizenship** - Proof of language proficiency
- **Professional Designation** - Some regulatory bodies accept CELPIP

## CELPIP vs IELTS

| Feature | CELPIP | IELTS |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Format | Computer-based | Paper or computer |
| Accent | Canadian/North American | British/International |
| Speaking | To computer | To examiner |
| Results | 4-5 business days | 13 days |
| Validity | 2 years | 2 years |

## Test Types

### CELPIP-General
All 4 components for immigration and citizenship:
- Listening (47-55 minutes)
- Reading (55-60 minutes)
- Writing (53-60 minutes)
- Speaking (15-20 minutes)

### CELPIP-General LS
Listening and Speaking only for citizenship:
- Listening (47-55 minutes)
- Speaking (15-20 minutes)

## Scoring

CELPIP uses levels from M (minimal) to 12 (advanced):

| Level | CLB Equivalent | Description |
|-------|---------------|-------------|
| 10-12 | CLB 10-12 | Advanced |
| 7-9 | CLB 7-9 | Adequate for most immigration |
| 4-6 | CLB 4-6 | Developing |
| M-3 | CLB 0-3 | Minimal |
