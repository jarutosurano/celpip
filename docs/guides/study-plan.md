## Overview

This plan is designed for **working professionals** with limited time.

| Day Type | Hours | Focus |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Weekdays | 2 hrs | Single skill focus |
| Weekends | 4-5 hrs | Mock tests & review |
| **Total** | **~18 hrs** | All 4 sections |

## Day-by-Day Plan

### Day 1 (Monday) - Listening

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning (30 min) | English podcast during commute |
| Lunch (15 min) | Review listening strategies |
| Evening (1.5 hrs) | 2 listening practice tests |

**Focus:** Note-taking, catching details on first listen

---

### Day 2 (Tuesday) - Reading

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning (30 min) | Skimming & scanning practice |
| Lunch (15 min) | Speed read an article |
| Evening (1.5 hrs) | 2 reading practice tests (timed) |

**Focus:** Time management, finding answers quickly

---

### Day 3 (Wednesday) - Writing

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning (30 min) | Study email templates |
| Lunch (15 min) | Review survey response structure |
| Evening (1.5 hrs) | Write 2 practice responses |

**Focus:** Structure and organization

---

### Day 4 (Thursday) - Speaking

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning (30 min) | Review all 8 task types |
| Lunch (15 min) | Practice Task 1-2 (record yourself) |
| Evening (1.5 hrs) | Record all 8 tasks, review recordings |

**Focus:** Fluency over perfection

---

### Day 5 (Friday) - Weak Areas

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning (30 min) | Review notes from the week |
| Lunch (15 min) | Vocabulary review |
| Evening (1.5 hrs) | Practice your 2 weakest sections |

**Focus:** Targeted improvement

---

### Day 6 (Saturday) - Full Mock Test

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning (3 hrs) | Complete full mock test |
| Afternoon (1.5 hrs) | Review all answers |
| Evening (30 min) | Strategy videos for weak areas |

**Focus:** Test simulation, stamina building

---

### Day 7 (Sunday) - Rest & Prep

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Morning (2 hrs) | Light review only |
| Afternoon | REST - No studying! |
| Evening (30 min) | Prepare logistics, sleep early |

**Focus:** Mental preparation

---

## Test Day Checklist

- [ ] Valid government-issued photo ID
- [ ] Know test center location
- [ ] Set multiple alarms
- [ ] Comfortable clothes
- [ ] 8+ hours of sleep
- [ ] Light breakfast
- [ ] Arrive 30 minutes early
