    >
    > In terms of the disruption this has caused, the most pressing issue is that the noise prevents me from sleeping on most weeknights, leading to fatigue and reduced productivity at work.     >

## Quick Reminders

| Rule | Remember |
|------|----------|
| **Salutation must match Sign-off** | Dear Sir → Yours Faithfully / Dear Mr. X → Sincerely / Hi Name → All the best |
| **Address all 3 bullet points** | Missing one drops your Task Fulfillment score |
| **Transition phrases never change** | "To briefly review..." → "In terms of..." → "Regarding..." → "Thank you in advance for..." |
| **Purpose verb matches email type** | Complaint → "express my dissatisfaction" / Request → "inquire about" / Suggestion → "offer a suggestion" |
| **Use Present Perfect for duration** | "I have been a resident for five years" (not "I am a resident since five years") |
