## Email Structure

Every email should have:

1. **Greeting** - Appropriate for the recipient
2. **Opening** - State your purpose
3. **Body** - Main content (2-3 paragraphs)
4. **Closing** - Call to action or wrap-up
5. **Sign-off** - Appropriate farewell

## Tone Guide

### Formal (Manager, Company, Official)
```
Dear Mr./Ms. [Name],
Dear Hiring Manager,
Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to inquire about...
I would like to request...
I am writing to express my concern regarding...

I look forward to your response.
Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Sincerely,
Best regards,
```

### Semi-Formal (Colleague, Acquaintance)
```
Hi [Name],
Hello [Name],

I wanted to reach out about...
I'm writing to let you know...
I hope this email finds you well.

Let me know if you have any questions.
Thanks for your help with this.

Best,
Thanks,
```

### Informal (Friend, Family)
```
Hey [Name]!
Hi there!

Just wanted to tell you...
Guess what happened!
How's it going?

Can't wait to hear from you!
Talk soon!

Cheers,
Take care,
```

## Common Email Scenarios

### 1. Complaint Email
```
Opening: I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with...
Body:
- Describe the problem
- Explain how it affected you
- State what you've already tried
Closing: I would appreciate if you could... / I expect a resolution by...
```

### 2. Request Email
```
Opening: I am writing to request...
Body:
- Explain why you need it
- Provide relevant details
- Mention any deadlines
Closing: I would be grateful if you could... / Please let me know if...
```

### 3. Apology Email
```
Opening: I am writing to apologize for...
Body:
- Take responsibility
- Explain (don't make excuses)
- State how you'll fix it
Closing: I hope you can understand... / Please accept my sincere apology.
```

### 4. Thank You Email
```
Opening: I wanted to thank you for...
Body:
- Be specific about what you're thankful for
- Explain the impact
- Mention future connection
Closing: Thanks again for... / I really appreciate...
```

## Time Management

| Phase | Time | Activity |
|-------|------|----------|
| Read & Plan | 3 min | Understand prompt, note key points |
| Write | 20 min | Follow structure, address all points |
| Review | 4 min | Check grammar, spelling, completeness |

## Checklist

Before submitting, verify:
- [ ] Addressed ALL parts of the prompt
- [ ] Appropriate tone for recipient
- [ ] Clear opening and closing
- [ ] 150-200 words
- [ ] No major spelling errors
- [ ] Proper paragraphing
