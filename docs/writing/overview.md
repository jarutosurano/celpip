## Test Structure

The Writing test has **2 tasks** and takes **53-60 minutes**.

## Task 1: Writing an Email (27 minutes)

You'll write an email based on a given situation.

### Types of Emails
- **Formal:** To a manager, company, official
- **Semi-formal:** To a colleague, acquaintance
- **Informal:** To a friend, family member

### What's Assessed
- Responding to all parts of the prompt
- Appropriate tone for the audience
- Clear organization
- Vocabulary and grammar
- Word count: ~150-200 words

## Task 2: Responding to Survey Questions (26 minutes)

You'll respond to a survey with your opinion on a topic.

### Format
- Choose ONE option from given choices
- Explain your choice
- Support with reasons and examples

### What's Assessed
- Clear opinion statement
- Logical supporting arguments
- Specific examples
- Coherent organization
- Word count: ~150-200 words

## Scoring Criteria

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 10-12 | Sophisticated vocabulary, complex sentences, minor errors |
| 7-9 | Good range of vocabulary, clear ideas, some errors |
| 4-6 | Basic vocabulary, simple sentences, noticeable errors |
| M-3 | Limited vocabulary, unclear ideas, many errors |

## Key Success Factors

!!! tip "Remember"

    1. **Answer ALL parts** of the prompt
    2. **Use appropriate tone** for the audience
    3. **Organize clearly** with paragraphs
    4. **Proofread** in the last 2-3 minutes
