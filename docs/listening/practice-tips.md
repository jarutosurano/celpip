## Daily Practice Routine

### Morning (15-30 min)
- Listen to English podcast during commute
- No subtitles or transcripts
- Focus on understanding the gist

### Evening (30-60 min)
- Complete 1-2 practice tests
- Review incorrect answers
- Re-listen to difficult sections

## Recommended Resources

### Podcasts
- **6 Minute English** (BBC) - Short, clear, British accent
- **All Ears English** - American accent, conversational
- **CBC Podcasts** - Canadian accent (most similar to CELPIP)

### News
- **CBC News** - Canadian content and accent
- **NPR** - American, clear pronunciation
- **BBC World Service** - International topics

## Active Listening Techniques

### Shadowing
1. Play audio at normal speed
2. Repeat what you hear immediately
3. Try to match pronunciation and rhythm
4. Helps with processing speed

### Dictation
1. Listen to a short clip (30-60 seconds)
2. Write down everything you hear
3. Check against transcript
4. Identify what you missed

### Summarizing
1. Listen to a 2-3 minute audio
2. Pause and summarize in your own words
3. Check if you captured main points
4. Repeat with longer content

## Trouble Areas

### Can't Catch Fast Speech?
- Start with slower content (audiobooks)
- Gradually increase speed
- Practice with real conversations

### Missing Details?
- Focus on note-taking
- Practice writing while listening
- Use abbreviations

### Losing Focus?
- Take breaks every 20-30 minutes
- Stay hydrated
- Practice in test-like conditions
