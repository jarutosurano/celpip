## Test Structure

The Listening test has **6 parts** and takes **47-55 minutes**.

!!! note
    Audio plays **only ONCE**. You cannot replay or pause.

## Part Breakdown

### Part 1: Listening to Problem Solving (8 questions)
- **Context:** Conversation about solving a problem
- **Length:** ~3-4 minutes
- **Tip:** Focus on the solution being discussed

### Part 2: Daily Life Conversation (5 questions)
- **Context:** Casual conversation between friends/colleagues
- **Length:** ~2-3 minutes
- **Tip:** Listen for opinions and attitudes

### Part 3: Listening for Information (6 questions)
- **Context:** Informational content (announcements, instructions)
- **Length:** ~2-3 minutes
- **Tip:** Take notes on specific details

### Part 4: Listening to a News Item (5 questions)
- **Context:** News report or broadcast
- **Length:** ~2 minutes
- **Tip:** Focus on who, what, when, where, why

### Part 5: Listening to a Discussion (8 questions)
- **Context:** Group discussion or meeting
- **Length:** ~4-5 minutes
- **Tip:** Track different speakers' viewpoints

### Part 6: Listening to Viewpoints (6 questions)
- **Context:** Multiple speakers sharing opinions
- **Length:** ~4-5 minutes
- **Tip:** Identify each speaker's position

## Scoring Criteria

Your listening is scored on:
- Comprehension of main ideas
- Understanding of specific details
- Ability to make inferences
- Understanding of speaker purpose and tone
