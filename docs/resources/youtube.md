## How to Use YouTube Effectively

### Do:
- Watch actively, take notes
- Pause and practice along
- Watch sample responses critically
- Use playback speed controls
- Create playlists by topic

### Don't:
- Just watch passively
- Skip practicing yourself
- Watch only one channel
- Memorize sample answers
- Substitute videos for practice tests
