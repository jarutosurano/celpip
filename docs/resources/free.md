## Tips for Using Free Resources

!!! tip "Maximize Free Resources"

    1. **Start with official materials** - Most accurate representation
    2. **Use multiple sources** - Different perspectives help
    3. **Practice under test conditions** - Time yourself
    4. **Review mistakes** - Learn from errors
    5. **Track your progress** - Note improvement areas

## Limitations of Free Resources

- May not match exact test format
- Limited quantity of materials
- AI scoring may not be 100% accurate
- Less comprehensive than paid options

If you have budget, consider supplementing with [paid resources](paid.md).
