### Celtestpip.com

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Questions | 3,000+ practice questions |
| Mock Exams | 50 full mock exams |
| Scoring | AI-powered instant scoring |

**Pros:**
- Real exam simulation
- Affordable weekly option (good for short-term prep)
- Comprehensive coverage

**Cons:**
- Some users report errors in reading section
- Limited user reviews

!!! note "Recommendation"

    Good for 1-2 week intensive prep. The weekly subscription is cost-effective for short study periods.

## Is Paid Worth It?

### When to Pay:
- You've exhausted free resources
- You need more practice tests
- You want detailed feedback
- You're preparing for a short time (1-2 weeks)

### When Free is Enough:
- You're already scoring 7+
- You have 4+ weeks to prepare
- You're good at self-study
- Budget is very limited

## Money-Saving Tips

1. **Start with free resources** - Only buy if needed
2. **Use weekly subscriptions** - Better for short prep
3. **Share study guides** - With study partners (if allowed)
4. **Watch for sales** - Official materials sometimes discounted
5. **Library resources** - Some libraries have CELPIP materials

---

## ROI Perspective

Consider:
- Test fee: ~$280-350 CAD
- Retake cost: Same amount + time

Investing $50-100 in preparation could save you hundreds if it means passing the first time.
