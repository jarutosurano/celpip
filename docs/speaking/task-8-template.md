## The Template

**Prep time:** 60 seconds | **Speaking time:** 60 seconds | **Blanks to fill:** 4

Everything outside the brackets is **memorized word for word**. Scan the picture and fill in what you see.

```
"Hi [NAME]. I know you have been looking for a
[THING] for a while now. Well, I am walking around
right now, and I haven't seen anything quite like
this before!

You won't believe this, but it is absolutely
stunning. It is quite sizable, and the whole thing
looks enchanting.

What really caught my eye is [DETAIL 1]. It is
truly magnificent. On top of that, [DETAIL 2]. The
attention to detail is really fascinating. It really
is a delightful setup.

I really think you should come and see this for
yourself. You would absolutely love it. Let me know
when you're free and we can come back together."
```

---

## Your 60-Second Prep

Scribble 4 things on your notepad:

```
1. name
2. looking for a ___
3. caught my eye — one thing
4. on top of that — one more thing
```

That's it. The adjectives and present perfect grammar are already memorized.

## Examples

### Candy Playground

**Prompt:** You discover a whimsical playground designed with a candy theme. Call your friend Sarah and describe it. Suggest she bring her children.

> "Hi Sarah. I know you have been looking for a **[playground for the kids]** for a while now. Well, I am looking at it right now, and I haven't seen anything quite like this before!
>
> You won't believe this, but it is absolutely stunning. It is quite sizable, and the whole thing looks enchanting.
>
> What really caught my eye is **[a giant play tower shaped like a cupcake with pink frosting on top]**. It is truly magnificent. On top of that, **[the stepping stones are designed to look like miniature Oreo cookies]**. The attention to detail is really fascinating. It really is a delightful setup.

> I really think you should come and see this for yourself. You would absolutely love it. Let me know when you're free and we can come back together."

## Delivery Tips — Sound Natural, Not Robotic

### Pause with Purpose

| Bad pause | Good pause |
|-----------|-----------|
| "um... you won't believe this..." | "You won't believe this... *(pause)* ...but it is absolutely stunning." |
| "uh... what caught my eye..." | "What really caught my eye is... *(pause)* ...[detail]." |

### Emphasize Key Words

| Line | How to say it |
|------|--------------|
| "I **haven't** seen anything quite like this!" | Sound excited, amazed |
| "absolutely **stunning**" | Slow down, sound impressed |
| "truly **magnificent**" | Sound genuinely wowed |
| "really **fascinating**" | Sound curious, intrigued |
| "You would absolutely **love** it" | Sound warm, inviting |

### The Secret: Sound Excited

Task 8 is a phone call to a friend or family member. Sound like you're genuinely amazed by what you see — not reading a report. Energy and excitement in your voice will push your score up.
