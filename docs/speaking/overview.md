## Test Structure

The Speaking test has **8 tasks** and takes **15-20 minutes**.

!!! note "Key Point"

    You speak into a microphone and your responses are recorded. There is no human examiner.

## Task Summary

| Task | Description | Prep | Speaking |
|------|-------------|------|----------|
| 1 | Giving Advice | 30s | 90s |
| 2 | Talking About Personal Experience | 30s | 60s |
| 3 | Describing a Scene | 30s | 60s |
| 4 | Making Predictions | 30s | 60s |
| 5 | Comparing and Persuading | 60s | 60s |
| 6 | Dealing with a Difficult Situation | 60s | 60s |
| 7 | Expressing Opinions | 30s | 90s |
| 8 | Describing an Unusual Situation | 30s | 60s |

## Scoring Criteria

You're assessed on:

### Content/Coherence
- Relevant response to the prompt
- Logical organization
- Clear main ideas and supporting details

### Vocabulary
- Range of words used
- Appropriate word choice
- Natural expressions

### Listenability
- Clear pronunciation
- Natural pace (not too fast/slow)
- Appropriate stress and intonation

### Task Fulfillment
- Completing the full task
- Addressing all parts
- Appropriate length

## Golden Rules

!!! tip "Remember These"

    1. **Never stay silent** - Keep talking even if imperfect
    2. **Don't translate** - Think in English
    3. **Use all your time** - Don't finish early
    4. **Be natural** - Speak like a real conversation
    5. **Practice aloud** - Silent practice doesn't help
