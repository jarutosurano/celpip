## Daily Reading Habit

### What to Read
- **News articles** - CBC, Globe and Mail, Toronto Star
- **Emails at work** - Practice reading quickly
- **Instructions/manuals** - Good for Part 2
- **Opinion pieces** - Good for Part 4

### How Much
- Minimum: 30 minutes/day
- Ideal: 1 hour/day
- Mix different types of content

## Speed Reading Practice

### Week 1-2: Build Foundation
- Read for 20 minutes without stopping
- Don't look up every unknown word
- Focus on understanding the gist

### Week 3-4: Increase Speed
- Time yourself reading articles
- Try to read 10% faster each week
- Use a pointer (finger or pen) to guide eyes

### Week 5+: Test Conditions
- Practice with real CELPIP questions
- Strict time limits
- No dictionary

## Vocabulary Building

### High-Frequency Words
Focus on words common in:
- Workplace communication
- News and current events
- Academic discussions

### Context Clues
Learn to guess meaning from:
- Surrounding sentences
- Word parts (prefixes, suffixes)
- Similar words you know

### Word Families
Learn related forms:
- decide → decision → decisive → indecisive
- employ → employee → employer → employment

## Self-Assessment

After each practice test, ask:
- [ ] Did I finish on time?
- [ ] Which part took longest?
- [ ] What question types did I miss?
- [ ] Did I understand main ideas?
- [ ] Could I identify author's purpose?

Track patterns to focus your study.
