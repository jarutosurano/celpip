## Test Structure

The Reading test has **4 parts** and takes **55-60 minutes**.

:::tip
Time management is crucial. Don't spend too long on any single question.
:::

## Part Breakdown

### Part 1: Reading Correspondence (11 questions)
- **Content:** Emails, letters, messages
- **Time:** ~12 minutes
- **Skills:** Understanding purpose, tone, specific details

### Part 2: Reading to Apply a Diagram (8 questions)
- **Content:** Text with visual information
- **Time:** ~10 minutes
- **Skills:** Connecting text to diagrams, charts, maps

### Part 3: Reading for Information (9 questions)
- **Content:** Informational articles, reports
- **Time:** ~12 minutes
- **Skills:** Finding specific information, understanding organization

### Part 4: Reading for Viewpoints (10 questions)
- **Content:** Opinion pieces, editorials
- **Time:** ~15 minutes
- **Skills:** Understanding arguments, identifying perspectives

## Question Types

| Type | Strategy |
|------|----------|
| Multiple choice | Eliminate wrong answers first |
| Drop-down menu | Read surrounding context |
| Matching | Scan for keywords |
| True/False/Not Given | Look for exact matches |

## Time Management
